Marjorie McNeely Conservatory

Opened to the public in 1915, the conservatory houses and maintains several garden environments including Japanese Gardens, Ferns, Sunken Gardens, and the fabulous Palm Dome. A Tropical Encounter exhibit features plants and animals from the tropics. It's open daily, year around.

The structure is on the National Register of Historic Places. For detailed history and hours of operation, check out the website at http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Como_Park_Zoo_and_Conservatory
