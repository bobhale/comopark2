Como Lakeside Pavilion

Perched on the shore of Lake Como, the pavilion, built in 1905 houses the Black Bear Crossings restaurant, an open-air concert hall, canoe and paddle boat rental with 1.75 miles of bike/pedestrian trails around the lake. Bike rentals are available.

Lake Como

The lake itself has its origin in the mile high glaciers that covered a large part of the midwest 10,000 years ago. The melt from the snow and ice created Lake Como as well as most other rivers and lakes in the area.

Fishing

The lake is stocked with several species of fish including pike and catfish. On the south shore there is a floating fishing pier where you can try your luck.
