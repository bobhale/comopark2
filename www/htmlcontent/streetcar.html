Historic Street Car Station

The Historic Streetcar Station is located at 1224 N. Lexington. It's distinctive rock-faced structure is visible from Como Avenue and Lexington Pkwy. It was built in 1905 for passengers traveling the street car line between Como Park and Lake Harriet in Minneapolis.

It now houses an interpretive museum about the Twin Cities Streetcar system and Como Park. The renovated building includes an interpretive exhibit area, public restrooms, office space, and an outdoor patio. Two historic bridges are located adjacent the structure.
