Black Bear Crossings

Located in the Historic Como Lakeside Pavilion, Black Bear Crossings offers everything from a gourmet cafe open year round, to free summer concerts and paddle boat rentals on the lake. Our signature community meeting rooms, free of charge, have hosted many groups over the years. For your wedding ceremony or reception, Black Bear Crossings offers simple elegance, inside or out. We welcome you to explore what Black Bear Crossings has to offer, and allow our professional and courteous staff to exceed your expectations!
