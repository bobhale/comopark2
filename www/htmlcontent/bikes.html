Bicycles

Trails

Numerous bike trails criss-cross the park and are accessible via other major bike paths and street lanes.

View them on the Maps tab or visit http://www.cyclopath.org/. One bit of caution, this is not a mobile site so viewing from a small screen may be difficult.

Bike Rental

NiceRide of Minnesota has installed a bike rack at Como Park Lakeside Pavilion. Bikes are available for short term rental in the park, or travel to other NiceRide locations.

Either download their app or visit http://www.niceridemn.org for details.
