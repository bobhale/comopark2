Shuttle
Avoid parking hassles with the free shuttle service. Shuttles operate weekends from May thru September 28. Daily service runs June through mid-August. 
There will be NO shuttle service June 20-22. 
Passengers board at the staffed off-site parking lot at the Minnesota State Fair Grounds*, located on the south side of Como Avenue across from the Warner Coliseum (the concrete hippodrome),and are dropped at front doors of the Visitor Center. The shuttle operates continuously from 9:30am to 6:30pm. 
Please Note: During the Minnesota State Fair, the shuttle operates from other locations TBA. View the News feed for up-to-date news. 
Accessibility: The buses are wheelchair and stroller accessible.
