Como Zoo

Open 365 days and accomodating over a million visitors each year makes Como Zoo favorite destination. Admission to the zoo is FREE however a $3.00 for Adults,$2.00 for Children donation is suggested. Hours are 10AM - 6PM May to October and 10AM - 4PM other times.

The Visitors Service Office provides information, first aid, public announcements, the reuniting of separated/lost individuals, and a lost and found department. Lockers are available for $.75. Stroller and wheelchair rental can also be found at this location.
