ADMISSION

Free. A voluntary donation of $3 for an adult and $2 for a child to help us care for our plants and animals is appreciated. You are always welcome whether or not you choose to make a donation.

HOURS

Open every day of the year. Summer hours (April - September) 10am - 6pm

Winter hours (October - March) 10am - 4pm

PARKING

Download Como Map for the location of our parking lots. There are a number of parking lots throughout the Como Regional Park area.

But, instead of searching for that elusive parking spot, we would like to encourage you to take the free shuttle. Shuttle service begins weekends April 20- September 29 (with the exception of August 24-25 and August 31-September 2 when there will be no service), and daily service begins June 1 - August 9. Click on the "Shuttle" tab for more information.

Beginning May 1st residential permit parking restrictions will be in effect. There will be no parking allowed on many streets west of Como Park from 10am-4pm, May 1-September 30 without a City issued permit.

DO NOT PARK on streets with signs indicating "Permit Parking Only"
