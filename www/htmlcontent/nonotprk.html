Parking

There are several lots available for parking throughout the park. Check out the map page for locations. If you are driving to the park you may want to consider taking the shuttle and save yourself the hassle of looking for a place for your vehicle.

No Parking

Beginning May 1st until September 30, residential parking restrictions will be in effect. Parking is not allowed on many of the streets west of the park area during the hours of 10AM to 4PM, without a valid permit. Watch for signs indicating "Permit Parking Only". Avoid the fines and watch for the signs!
